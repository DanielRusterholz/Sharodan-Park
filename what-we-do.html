What We Do

Nature Walks and Trails

Sharodan Park offers peaceful walking along creeks, through forests, rolling hills, and open fields. Reconnect with the quiet beauty of nature as you explore the wonders around you.

Special Events

We offer Pathfinder Clubs special events like camping and abseiling and Camporee.

Workshops and Learning

We believe in healthy living and therefore use and preserve as much of our own produce as possible. if you want to learn how to preserve food, we do have workshops on food preservation

Peaceful Retreats

Sharodan Park is a sanctuary for the soul. Whether you're seeking a quiet place to reflect, pray, or simply rest, our peaceful surroundings offer space for true renewal.
